Thumper is a fictional charectar from Disneys animated film Bambi from 1942. He is known for his habit of thumping his left hind foot

The charectar was an important influence upon the development of the new movie Bambi. Thumper was expanded from its original minor role because it was considered fun and entertaining by the movie creators.
